About

At our core, we prioritize pushing boundaries, embracing the unknown, and fostering a culture of continuous learning.

FGEN (Future Generation) is dedicated to enlightening the next generation about the marvels of the human brain. Our mission is to cultivate a deep understanding and appreciation of neuroscience among young minds, empowering them to become informed, compassionate leaders. By providing accessible education, fostering curiosity, and promoting awareness, we aim to inspire future generations to leverage their knowledge of the brain to innovate, lead with empathy, and create a brighter, more informed world. Through FGEN, we are committed to nurturing a community where the study of the brain ignites a passion for lifelong learning and leadership.
